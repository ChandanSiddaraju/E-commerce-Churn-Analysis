## 📌 Overview  
This project focuses on analyzing **customer churn** in an **e-commerce platform**, identifying key factors behind attrition, and predicting churn using **machine learning models**. The goal is to provide actionable insights to improve customer retention strategies.  

## 📊 Key Insights  
- **16.84% churn rate** – indicating room for retention improvements.  
- **High churn in the first 18 months** – requires better onboarding.  
- **Female customers have higher retention** than male customers.  
- **Spending drops around 30 months** – needs re-engagement strategies.  
- **923 customers predicted to churn** based on model analysis.  

## 🎯 Business Recommendations  
✅ **Improve Onboarding & Early Engagement** – Offer personalized support & discounts to new customers.  
✅ **Targeted Retention Strategies** – Focus on male customers & high-spenders.  
✅ **Competitor & Pricing Analysis** – Adapt pricing strategies in low-satisfaction regions.  
✅ **Loyalty Programs** – Reward long-term customers to sustain engagement.  

## 🛠 Tech Stack  
- **Python (pandas, NumPy, matplotlib, seaborn, scikit-learn)**  
- **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) & Feature Engineering**  
- **Machine Learning Models: Logistic Regression, Decision Trees**  
- **Evaluation Metrics: Accuracy, Precision, Recall, AUC-ROC**
